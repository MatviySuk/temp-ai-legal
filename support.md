## Common questions

### How do I get started?

After downloading the app, sign in with Apple. You'll be asked to either create a new group or join an existing one with an invite code. Tasks, expenses, shopping lists, and saved places are all shared within your group.

### Why does the app need so many permissions?

- **Microphone** — to capture your voice commands for the AI assistant
- **Speech Recognition** — to transcribe your speech locally on the device
- **Notifications** — to remind you about tasks at a set time or when you arrive at a place
- **Location ("While Using" or "Always")** — to trigger location-based reminders (e.g. "remind me to buy milk when I'm at the supermarket")

All voice processing happens on your device. Audio is never sent to our servers or to any third party.

### How do I invite someone to my group?

Go to **Settings → Group → Generate Invite Code**. Share the 6-character code with the person you want to invite. The code is valid for 24 hours. They paste it into the **Join Group** screen on first launch.

### Does the AI assistant work offline?

Yes. The AI runs entirely on your device. The first time you use it, the app downloads a language model (~1.9 GB) over Wi-Fi. After that, voice commands work offline.

### Can I use the AI in English?

Yes. Go to **Settings → AI Assistant → Language** and pick English. The same model supports both Ukrainian and English voice commands.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings → Delete Account**. This permanently removes your email, name, and user ID from our database. If you're the sole owner of a group, the group and all its content are deleted too. If the group has other owners or members, content you created is reassigned to another active owner so the group's history is preserved.

### Why don't I see notifications when tasks are due?

Make sure notifications are enabled in **iOS Settings → temp-ai → Notifications**. iOS limits how many location-based reminders an app can monitor at once — we monitor up to 20 places.

### The app crashed — what now?

Please email us at **matvii.suk.se@gmail.com** with a brief description of what you were doing. Crash reports are also sent to our error-tracking system automatically (anonymously), so we may already be aware of the issue.
